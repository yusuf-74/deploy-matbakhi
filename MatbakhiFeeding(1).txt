What is Matbakhi?
Matbakhi is a hotel cloud kitchen company in Saudi Arabia.

Matbakhi vision:
To be the most sought-after house of innovative brands powered by the largest hotel-cloud kitchens network in the world

To create memorable food experiences; one meal a time
